Contemporary Art Museum of Oakland

The Contemporary Art Museum Oakland is an open curatorial collective exhibiting 2D, 3D, and 4D artwork in the OMNI common areas.

CAMO combines internally generated shows, calls for entry and guest curators to bring high quality conceptual/visual content to the walls of OMNI.
